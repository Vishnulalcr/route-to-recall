PDF FILES PLACEHOLDER
=====================

Place your PDF files in this directory:

1. terms-and-conditions.pdf - Full Terms and Conditions document
2. cancellation-refunds.pdf - Full Cancellation and Refunds policy document

These PDFs will be downloadable from the respective policy pages.

Current setup:
- Terms page: /app/terms/page.tsx (links to /documents/terms-and-conditions.pdf)
- Cancellation page: /app/cancellation/page.tsx (links to /documents/cancellation-refunds.pdf)
